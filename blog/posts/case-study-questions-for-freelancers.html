Case Study Questions for Freelancers

Use these case study questions to turn finished client work into stronger proof for future sales.

Why this matters

Finished projects are the best moment to capture proof. The client remembers the problem, the delivery is fresh, and a clean process makes you look organized instead of desperate.

Simple workflow

  1. Confirm what was completed.
  2. Send final assets and usage notes.
  3. Ask for a short testimonial with a prompt.
  4. Ask for one referral with a forwardable intro.
  5. Capture case-study details while context is fresh.
  6. Suggest the most logical next step.
  7. Schedule a follow-up reminder.

Copyable script

“I’m glad we were able to complete this project. If it was helpful, would you be open to sending 2–3 sentences I can use as a testimonial?”
