The 7-step offboarding checklist

Use this when a project is complete, the client is satisfied, and momentum is still warm.

1. Confirm the win

Summarize what shipped and what result the work supports.

clarity

2. Deliver assets

Link final files, access notes, and usage instructions.

handoff

3. Ask for testimonial

Ask while the value is fresh and give a prompt.

social proof

4. Ask for referral

Name the kind of person you help and give a forwardable intro.

pipeline

5. Capture case study

Collect before/after context, process, and measurable result.

portfolio

6. Offer next step

Suggest maintenance, a follow-up sprint, or a check-in.

7. Schedule follow-up

Set a 30/60/90-day reminder.
